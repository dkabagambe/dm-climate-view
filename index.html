About DM Climateview UG LTD

DM ClimateView UG LTD is a non-profit organization that was founded in February 2020 by a group of passionate climate change activists from Makerere University. In May 2023, it obtained official registration with the Uganda Registration Services Bureau (URSB)

The primary focus of DM ClimateView UG LTD is to raise awareness about climate change , provide research,and advocate for action. They achieve this through organizing events, campaigns, and providing resources that empower individuals to take meaningful steps towards addressing climate change. By engaging with the community and sharing knowledge, the organization aims to contribute to a more sustainable future.
